Our Mission

The Appalachian Rescue Coalition is fundraising to become an animal rescue and adoption organization serving South-Eastern Kentucky. Our mission is to save abandoned, neglected, and stray animals seeking a second chance at a forever home. We work to secure a better future for them and provide support to the animals that remain in our care.

Please Consider Donating

The Appalachian Rescue Coalition is working to become a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization that will provide rescue to abandoned animals. We will work to find homes for abandoned dogs and cats; owned pets whose owners can no longer care for them; and animals who have been injured, abused, and neglected. Please consider donating today!

Every dollar raised will help:
• File documents that establish our existence as a corporate organization.
• Register our federal identification number issued by the IRS to identify us as a business entity.
• Research and prepare our business's license requirements to stay compliant.
• Comply with state Insurance filing requirements.
• Comply with state Income Tax Withholding filings.
• Comply with sales & Use Tax ID requirements.
• Comply with employer compliance requirements.
• File the 501(c)(3) application for the IRS to receive charitable contributions from donors. .
